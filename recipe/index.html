Oven-Baked Salmon

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillet
  • Old Bay Seasoning - to taste
  • Salt - to taste
  • Pepper - to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Season the salmon with the aforementioned ingredients.
  3. Bake Salmon for 15-20 minutes.
