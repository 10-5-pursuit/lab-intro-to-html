Recipe

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 butter knife
  • 2 slices of whole wheat breat

Directions

  1. First spread peanut butter on 1 slice. Make sure you put on enough, like half-inch, up to the crust.
  2. Wipe butter knife on the other piece of bread. Now it could be used to dip into the jelly.
  3. Now scoop out similar amount of jelly on the other piece. Do not spread smoothing jelly out to being even and thin. It's preferred to spread with globs.
  4. Take the bread piece with peanut butter and place it on top of the jelly piece.
  5. Tap sandwich gently for binding. Once complete, cut with butter knife in half. Enjoy!
