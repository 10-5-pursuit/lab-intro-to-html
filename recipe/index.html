Merinque Cookies

Description

These cookies are light and airy only take just a few ingredients to make.

Ingredients

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • a pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Preheat an oven to 225Â°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Combine egg whites, cream of tartar, salt and vanilla to a stand mixing bowl
  3. Using a whisk attachment beat the mixture until foamy
  4. Add the sugar a tablespoon at a time, beating the eggs on high until stiff peaks form
  5. Place your meringue in a piping bag and pipe out cookies around 1 inch in diameter
  6. Bake in the oven for 1 hr
  7. Once done turn off the oven and leave inside to cool before removing
