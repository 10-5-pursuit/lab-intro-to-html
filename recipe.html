The Perfect PB&J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and tastes great when toasted.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay 2 slices of bread facing towards you.
  2. Next, you use the butter knife to scoop and spread the peanut butter on one slice and the jelly on the other.
  3. Then, you bring the 2 slices together (with the peanut butter side and the jelly side facing each other).
  4. OPTIONAL: If you like your sandwhiches warm, you can toast your sandwich on both sides inside of a pan with a teaspoon of butter.
